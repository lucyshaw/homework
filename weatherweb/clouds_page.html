"I'm Just Happy to See the Sun"

Latitude vs. Cloudiness Analysis


There was no visible correlation between latitude and cloudiness. Which is probably expected. We also see that there is a "banding" of data points at certain percents of cloudiness. This implies that the measure of cloudiness is perhaps a subjective one, and therefore more apt to be rounded or reported in whole or round percentages. Cloudiness ranges from 0%, meaning a bright, blue skied day, all the way to 100% as would be expected on a cloud covered, grey day.
